Checkers Delights

Welcome to Checkers Delight, an interactive browser game that challenges players in a game of your classic checkers.

Rules of the Game

  • Each player begins the game with 12 discs.
  • A player can only move their disc diagonally and are always limited to forward moves.
  • A piece making a non capturing move may only move one square.
  • To capture a piece of your opponent, your piece leaps over one of the opponent's pieces and lands in a straight diagonal line on the other side. This landing square must be empty.
  • When a piece is captured it is removed from the board.
  • When a piece reaches the furthest row, it is crowned and becomes a king. Kings are limited to moving diagonally but can move both forward and backward.
  • A winner is declared when the opponent runs out of moves, or if all of his pieces have been captured.
